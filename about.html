Our Mission

Baja Love Outreach exists to empower the lives of children and staff in Baja California orphanages through supplemental aid and tangible love.

Christmas in July

We provide school supplies, back packs, uniforms and shoes to every school age child at every orphanage we are currently working with, to include the staff's children.

Christmas

Every orphan is given a gift of their choosing, as well as clothing and essentials.

Special needs orphanages

We will be providing wheelchairs, food, diapers and other critical needs for two orphanages in Maneadero, Mexico. As well as hands on relief for their staff.

B. Love Boutique

When you shop at the B. Love Boutique, you aren't just accessorising yourself, you are also donating to the well being of a small child in need.

Fundraising

We are currently having multiple fundraising events to help support future trips and aid for the 5 orphanages we are currently providing assistance for. There are multiple ways for you to get involved. Please click here to find out how.
